About Us

Oleander cooking website is where you can find out variety of cooking recipes.

We have different categories of traditional dishes, drinks and desserts which you can choose any you like.




Our website contain traditional dishes from different countries, different kinds of drinks and different kinds of desserts.


We have traditional dishes of six countries (Myanmar, Thailand, India, China, Korea, Japan) from Asia and three countries (the UK, French, Italy) from Europe.

We have four different categories (coffee, smoothies, cocktails, mocktails) under drinks.

We have four different categories (strawberry, chocolate, yogurt, vanilla) under desserts.
